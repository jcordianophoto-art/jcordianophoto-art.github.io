About

A different
eye for the game

I'm Julian Cordiano — a photographer based in Canada with a deep love for the game of golf and the visual stories it tells. My work gravitates toward the intimate, the atmospheric, and the overlooked: the shadow of a flagstick, light breaking through a canopy of trees, a player framed by the landscape around them.

The result is imagery that feels editorial rather than promotional — work that captures not just a golf course, but the feeling of being there. I'm available for commercial assignments, media partnerships, and collaborative shoots at courses across North America.
